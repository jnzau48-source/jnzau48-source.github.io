Our Mission

J-Tech Foundation is dedicated to supporting disaster-affected communities, improving daily life challenges, and helping the vulnerable including flood victims, orphans, street children, and fire outbreak survivors.

Current Challenges

Floods in Nairobi

Floods are affecting lives in Nairobi. Your support helps build drainage systems, evacuate the affected, and provide food & clean water.

Orphans & Street Children

Support orphanages and street kids through donations for education, food, and safe shelter.

Street Children

Help street children find hope and resources for a better life.

Fire Outbreaks

Provide emergency support to fire victims through timely aid and relief.

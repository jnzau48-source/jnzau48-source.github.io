Our Mission

J-Tech Foundation exists to provide immediate support to disaster victims, empower orphanages, assist street children, and create a stronger, safer community through your support.

Current Challenges

Floods

Floods in Nairobi have displaced families and damaged infrastructure. Your support can help build drainage systems, evacuate victims, and provide clean water.

Orphanages

Orphans need shelter, food, and education. Contributions allow us to provide a safe environment and improve their quality of life.

Street Children

Street children face neglect and hunger daily. With your help, we provide food, mentorship, and rehabilitation programs.

Fire Outbreaks

Fire disasters destroy homes and livelihoods. Donations allow us to respond quickly and provide essential aid to affected families.
